Nuba

uba is an international restaurant placed in a spectacular masia- typical country house - remodeled in a contemporary style, with a splendid terraNce and an attached porch.

Nuba's gastronomic offer is synonymous of quality, variety and creativity, and it is based on market cuisine, with a selection of dishes specially made for the most demanding palates, always carefully presented, from the simplest and lightest to the most sophisticated. Nuba is the place to be and the meeting point for the most exclusive high-end clientele in barcelona.

Restaurant service is until 12am when open the doors to public and turns into a pub/club.

Location: Les Corts district

Dining room capacity: 2 rooms for 60 and 130 people

Price Level: high

Style: Mediterranean

Vegetarian Options/Special diets: Available
