Tragaluz

Tragaluz restaurant is ocated at L'example district in a private and quiet passage that connects Passeig de Gràcia and Rambla Catalunya

Opened since 1989, Tragaluz is its flagship restaurant. Located on the top floor of El Japonés, the restaurant has a complete renovation since 2011 that makes it even more attractive.

It has a movable glass roof that allows to enjoy lunches and dinners under the sun or in the moonlight. The first level has a wonderful open kitchen, while the second is also perfect to taste exquisite cocktails.

It is a modern Mediterranean-inspired cuisine made with fresh ingredients and always well presented..

Location: Eixample Esquerra district

Dining room capacity: 3 floors from 20 to 80 people

Price Level: high

Style: Mediterranean

Vegetarian Options/Special diets: Available

Handicapped facilities: Yes
